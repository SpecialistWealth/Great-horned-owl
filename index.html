Welcome to the World of the Great Horned Owl

The Great Horned Owl (Bubo virginianus), also known as the tiger owl (originally derived from early naturalists' description as the "winged tiger" or "tiger of the air") or the hoot owl, is a large owl native to the Americas. It is an extremely adaptable bird with a vast range and is the most widely distributed true owl in the Americas. Its primary diet includes rabbits, hares, rats, mice, voles, and even larger animals such as mid-sized mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and invertebrates.
